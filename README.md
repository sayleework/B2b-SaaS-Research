# B2b-SaaS-Research

Chose the third topic: AI powered SEO content production

This research is focused on AI-powered SEO content production as a growth channel for B2B SaaS companies in the HR/recruiting space.

Will research across youtube, linkedin, spotify (for podcasts) and ofcourse google xray search for experts who practice what they preach.

Will then build different files to store all their names, content urls & content transcripts.

Research Methodology:
- Searched YouTube for terms like "AI SEO content", "programmatic SEO AI", "B2B SaaS SEO"
- Prioritized creators who show real workflows, tools, and results — not just theory
- Looked for experts across three levels: B2B SaaS SEO broadly, AI content production, and general SEO practitioners whose methods apply to SaaS
- Cross-referenced names that appeared across multiple searches or were mentioned by other experts
